Say "Hi" to Routy!

...the right route for many places.

What is it?

Routy is an app that helps you figure out the best route between many different destinations. Just give it an origin and up to 5 destinations and Routy will calculate the most efficient route by distance or time beginning at your origin! It's fast, easy, and really simple - jump right in and get Routing!

List of features...

  • Origin and 5 destinations route finding that's fast and easy.
  • Select between the most shortest distance or fastest time.
  • Don't know an exact address? Don't worry! We can help you figure it out.
  • Uses GPS to locate you if you want to use your current location as the origin.
  • Shows you a general map with your origin and destinations while linked to your Android maps app for detailed routes for each segment of your trip.
  • A beautiful, clean interface with its own style that helps you along without getting in your way.

Some technical stuff

  • Dynamic list building for pretty screens!
  • XML/JSON parsing for API calls.
  • Integration with both Google Directions and Google Places API.
  • Error messages built using Android Fragments (ICS) for helpful error handling.
  • Saving state between screens and when the app is loaded.
  • Custom logos, splash screens, buttons, pins, and tags.
